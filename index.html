How to use fplGraphed

1. Find your mini-league code:
To do this, go to your mini-league's page on fantasy.premierleague.com.
Your mini-league code is the number in the URL.
For the example below, the league code would be '314'.
fantasy.premierleague.com/leagues/314/standings/c

2. Enter your league code on the dashboard page:
Go to the dashboard page. To do this, you can:
Click on MENU >> DASHBOARD or scroll up and click on "GO TO DASHBOARD".
Then simply enter your mini-league code.

3. Visualise your mini-league:
Give the dashboard a moment to load your mini-league's data.
Once it has, have a look around all of the features listed in the section below!

4. Feedback and donate!
If you have any suggestions or experience any issues with the website please send these through to:
contact-us@fplgraphed.com
And finally, if you like the (free!) service, any support you can offer is much appreciated! ;)

fplGraphed features

See all of fplGraphed's features described below.
Alternatively, go to the dashboard, enter your league code and navigate around!

  • League Table

    View an enhanced league table for your mini-league which, in addition to showing participants' points, shows their overall worldwide rank, points lag from 1st place and form over the past 5 gameweeks, amongst other statistics.

  • Rankings

    See how participants in your mini-league have progressed gameweek by gameweek. There are two graphs: one showing mini-league rank and another showing overall worldwide rank.

  • Points

    Visualise how your mini-league has progressed. This charts participants' progress against whoever is top of the mini-league.

  • 1 v 1

    Compare any two players in your mini-league to see in which gameweeks the most ground has been won/lost. You may also visualise any one player's gameweek points across the season in one place here.

  • Bench Points

    Understand how many points have been squandered on your mini-league's benches. This section charts how many points players have on their benches as well as an alternative 'Points' graph which includes bench points.

  • Transfers

    Visualise the number of transfers made by players in your mini-league as well as the cumulative cost spent on hits.

  • Team Value

    See how team value across participants in your mini-league has evolved over the gameweeks.
