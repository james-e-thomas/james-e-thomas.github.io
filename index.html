How to use fplGraphed

1. Find your FPL code:
To do this, go to your 'Points' tab on fantasy.premierleague.com.
Your code is the first number in the URL.
For the example below, the code would be '123456'.
[...].com/entry/123456/event/19

2. Enter your FPL code on the dashboard page:
Go to the dashboard page. To do this, you can:
Click on MENU >> DASHBOARD or scroll up and click on "GO TO DASHBOARD".
Then simply enter your fpl code.

3. Visualise your mini-leagues:
Give the dashboard a moment to load your mini-leagues.
Select the mini-league you want to view.
Have a look around all of the features listed in the section below!

4. Bookmark your league and download the app:
To bookmark your code or league, navigate to the following page (add your own details as relevant):
https://www.fplgraphed.com/dashboard.html?player_code=123456&league_select=1
If you are browsing on mobile, you can save the website to your home screen and use it as you would an app.
Go to https://www.fplgraphed.com/index.html, click on your browser's share button and select "Add to Home Screen".
Unfortunately, this has to be done from the general URL, this will not work from your bookmarked URL.

5. Feedback and donate!
If you have any suggestions or experience any issues with the website please send these through to:
contact-us@fplgraphed.com
And finally, if you like the (free!) service, any support you can offer is much appreciated! ;)

fplGraphed features

See all of fplGraphed's features described below.
Alternatively, go to the dashboard, enter your code and navigate around!

  • League Table

    View an enhanced league table for your mini-league which, in addition to showing participants' points, shows their overall worldwide rank, points lag from 1st place and form over the past 5 gameweeks, amongst other statistics.

  • Live GW

    Look at how participants in your league are doing in the current gameweek. This shows live figures, excluding bonus points. See also how many players each participant still has to play.

  • Rankings

    See how participants in your mini-league have progressed gameweek by gameweek. There are two graphs: one showing mini-league rank and another showing overall worldwide rank.

  • Points

    Visualise how your mini-league has progressed. This charts participants' progress against whoever is top of the mini-league.

  • 1 v 1

    Compare any two players in your mini-league to see in which gameweeks the most ground has been won/lost. You may also visualise any one player's gameweek points across the season in one place here.

  • Bench Points

    Understand how many points have been wasted on your mini-league's benches. This section charts how many points players have on their benches as well as an alternative 'Points' graph which includes bench points.

  • Transfers

    Visualise the number of transfers made by players in your mini-league as well as the cumulative cost spent on hits.

  • Team Value

    See how team value across participants in your mini-league has evolved over the gameweeks.
